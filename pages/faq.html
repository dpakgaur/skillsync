Getting started with SkillSync is easy! First, create your account and complete your profile. Then navigate to the Dashboard where you can begin adding your skills, experiences, and projects. Our intuitive interface guides you through each step, and you can start building your professional portfolio right away.
Go to the Dashboard and open the Skills tab. Use the "Add New Skill" form to input your skills with proficiency levels. You can categorize skills (technical, soft skills, languages), set proficiency ratings, and add certifications. Skills can be edited or removed at any time, and they automatically sync with your portfolio.
